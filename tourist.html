Tourist Destination

Al-Akbar Mosque

As one of the biggest mosque in Indonesia, Al-Akbar mosque become one of the most popular destination here. Not just the humongous size, it's also has a magnificent yet elegant style. With their great vertical dome and the tower that has a height up to 99 meters making this destination must-go at Surabaya. Al-Akbar Mosque has a wide of a whole 22.300 m2 with 147 of length and 128 of weight, making it the second biggest mosque in Indonesia after Istiqlal Mosque. This mosque also has a unique dome that are similar to half-egg. And not just that, we can also go up to the tower to see Surabaya city in the 100 meters high. And at night, this mosque also has a colorful lights shining all over it's surrounding.

Surabaya Zoo

Surabaya Zoo, also known as Kebun Binatang Surabaya (KBS), is a popular attraction that offers varying animals to be seen. Also Surabaya Zoo is the only zoo in Surabaya. It's environment is green and calming making it a good choice to refresh our day. Lions, Zebra, Peacock and other can be found here. Not just animals, they also have several food stalls and restaurant waiting to be tried. There is also a park there that contains many trees, so even the Surabaya is very hot, it still can be enjoyed under the shade of their tree.
