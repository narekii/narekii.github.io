Local Food

Surabaya, the capital city in East Java, offers great food because many people come and go throughout this city. The cuisine there is varying from east to west. However, We do still have our mouth-watering delicacy. We name it Rujak Cingur and Lontong Balap. Here are the most dishes that we are proud of.

Rujak Cingur

Rujak cingur is a traditional Indonesian very rich in flavors and textures. It consists of cingur which is a cow's snout that is already marinated with spices, and much of salad vegetables. Then pour it with peanut sauce which has sweet, sour, and spicy flavors. The key to that is the ingredients which are ground peanuts, tamarind, chili peppers, and other spices. This combination creates a bursting experience for our tongues.

Lontong Balap

And then we also have Lontong Balap, a dish that features rice cakes (lontong), bean sprouts, tofu, and much more toppings that consist of shrimp crackers, eggs, and vegetables. The top of this dish is also poured with flavorful broth made of spices like shallots, garlic, pepper, and much more. Also don't forget about spicy sambal. These ingredients combination are enough to make our stomach gnawing in hunger.
